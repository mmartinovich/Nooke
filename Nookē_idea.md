Nookē
A Calm Way to Feel Close to Friends
An app that helps you feel connected without having to talk, post, or perform.

The Core Idea
Most social apps ask you to do things:
post, reply, react, explain yourself.
This app asks for nothing.
You don’t need to message anyone.
You don’t need to be “on.”
You just exist — and so do your people.

Why This Exists
Loneliness is high, but social media feels exhausting.
People want connection, but reaching out feels like pressure:
What do I say?

Do I reply now?

Am I being awkward?

So people scroll instead of connecting — and feel more alone.
This app removes that pressure entirely.

What This App Is
It’s an ambient presence app for close friends.
You add only 8–12 people you genuinely care about.
When you open the app, you see them as soft glowing dots:
If someone is online, their dot glows brighter

Colors show how they’re feeling

That’s it.
No feed.
No posts.
No likes.
You can open the app, glance, and close it.

How You Can Gently Connect (Optional)
You never have to interact — but you can, quietly.
Nudge

A silent “thinking of you.” No typing. No reply expected.

Open Rooms

Drop-in voice spaces. No calling. No ringing. Muted by default.

Perfect for quiet company or studying together. Or even gaming with friends.

Flare

A simple signal that says: “I need someone.”

No explanation required.

Everything is designed to feel low-effort and safe.

Who This Is For
People who love their friends but hate constant chatting

Anxious texters

Introverts

Burnt-out social media users

Long-distance friends

ADHD students (body-doubling)

Night owls who just want to know someone else is awake

The Moment That Matters
You open the app.
You see someone you care about online.
You think:
“I’m not alone.”
You close the app.
That feeling — that small warmth — is the product.

Why This Is Different
Most apps are about sharing your life.
This one is about not feeling alone.
No performance

No pressure

Silence is normal

Small, intimate circle only

It’s not social media.
It’s quiet togetherness.

Safety, by Design
Because this app is personal, safety is built in from the start:
Small friend limit

Quiet interaction limits

Silent blocking (no drama)

Pause / ghost modes

Trusted people you can rely on

The goal is to make connection feel safe and calm, not overwhelming.

One-Line Summary
Know your people are out there. That’s enough.
